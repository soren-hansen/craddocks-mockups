We Understand What Each Facility Type Actually Requires

The documentation requirements for a corporate office are different from a medical facility. The turnaround needs of a venue are different from a gym. We build a protocol specific to your facility — not a template.

🏢Corporate Offices & Campus Facilities

Arlington hosts major corporate employers and office parks. We run janitorial contracts with consistent crew assignments, supervisor-inspected shifts, and weekly reporting that goes to the facilities manager — not just a general inbox.

  • Assigned crew — same team every visit
  • Supervisor inspection checklist, signed off per shift
  • Digital log emailed to facilities manager same night
  • Common areas, conference rooms, restrooms, kitchens, lobbies

🏥Medical Facilities & Outpatient Clinics

Medical offices in Arlington face Joint Commission Environment of Care reviews and OSHA compliance requirements. Our documentation covers those requirements specifically — not a generic "medical cleaning" claim.

  • EPA-registered product records, by chemical and application area
  • EPA-registered disinfection — exam tables, high-touch zones — product records logged
  • Before/after photos on medical equipment cleaning
  • EOC-compliant cleaning logs emailed after every visit

🏋️Fitness Centers & Gyms

Fitness facilities in Arlington open early and close late. Cleaning windows are tight. We work within your schedule — pre-open, post-close — and clean to a standard that gym members notice.

  • Equipment wipe-down with EPA-registered disinfectants
  • High-traffic locker rooms and restrooms — EPA-registered disinfection logged
  • Flexible scheduling: early morning, late night, split shifts
  • Digital log emailed to GM after every visit

Documentation & Compliance

Every Visit Is Audit-Ready

Lancaster area facilities — medical offices, industrial sites, schools — face real regulatory requirements. Our documentation is built to meet those requirements, not to satisfy a cleaning company's internal checklist.

🏥 Joint Commission EOC

For medical clients: EPA-registered product records, before/after photographic documentation for medical equipment cleaning, and Environment of Care-compliant cleaning logs — all emailed after every visit.

⚠️ OSHA Compliance

Monthly OSHA safety training records for all crew. SDS sheets on-site for every chemical used. PPE documentation. Incident logs. Equipment inspection records maintained and available to clients.

🧪 ATP Surface Testing

High-touch surfaces tested with ATP bioluminescence meters after cleaning. Results logged, not estimated. If a surface doesn't pass, it gets re-cleaned before we leave. Result included in your digital log.

📋 Texas Regulatory Standards

We maintain compliance with Texas Department of State Health Services guidelines for commercial cleaning in healthcare-adjacent facilities and licensed daycare environments in Lancaster and surrounding cities.

📸 Photographic Documentation

Before/after photos on all medical equipment cleaning, floor restoration projects, and post-construction work. Photos time-stamped and included in the client digital log.

📊 Digital Cleaning Logs

Every visit produces a digital log: completed checklist, supervisor sign-off, ATP results, compliance status. Emailed to the facility manager the same night. Stored for your records.
